# Day 3 – Role-Based Prompting

## Objective

Learn how Role-Based Prompting changes the quality and usefulness of AI responses.

---

## What is Role-Based Prompting?

Role-Based Prompting is a prompt engineering technique where a specific role or persona is assigned to the AI before asking a question.

Example:

You are a Senior Software Engineer with 10 years of experience.

This allows the AI to answer from a specific perspective rather than giving a general response.

---

## Prompt Without Role

### Prompt

How can I improve my Book Library project to make it more attractive for recruiters?

### Response Characteristics

- Generic suggestions
- Broad advice
- Less focused
- Covers multiple areas but lacks depth

---

## Prompt With Role

### Prompt

You are a Senior Software Engineer with 10 years of experience.

How can I improve my Book Library project to make it more attractive for recruiters?

Give practical suggestions that will increase portfolio value.

### Response Characteristics

- Technical improvements
- Deployment suggestions
- Better coding practices
- Industry best practices
- Actionable recommendations

---

## Comparison

| Without Role | With Role |
|-------------|-----------|
| Generic answer | Expert-level answer |
| Broad advice | Specific guidance |
| Less focused | Goal-oriented |
| Limited depth | Technical depth |

---

## Key Learning

Assigning a role changes the AI's perspective and produces more specialized, relevant, and practical responses.

Small changes in prompts can significantly improve the quality of AI-generated outputs.
