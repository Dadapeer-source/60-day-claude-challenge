## Objective

Learn how AI can improve resume quality, ATS compatibility, and recruiter readability.

## Resume Analysis

I uploaded my existing resume and used AI to analyze it for:

* ATS Compatibility
* Resume Structure
* Keyword Optimization
* Recruiter Readability
* Professional Presentation

---

## Improvements Suggested

* Improved professional summary
* Better section organization
* ATS-friendly formatting
* Stronger action verbs
* Better keyword optimization
* Reduced redundancy

---

## Key Learning

A resume should be written not only for recruiters but also for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS).

Even strong skills and projects can be overlooked if the resume structure is not ATS-friendly.

---

## Biggest Insight

The most effective resumes are simple, keyword-optimized, achievement-focused, and easy for both ATS software and recruiters to read.
