# Day 22 - Startup Validation Report

## Overview

This project focuses on validating a startup idea using AI-driven market research and venture capital style analysis.

## Objective

Generate a professional startup validation report by evaluating:

- Executive Summary
- Problem Validation
- Founder-Market Fit
- TAM, SAM, SOM Analysis
- Competitor Analysis
- Market Gap
- Customer Persona
- Risk Assessment
- Pivot Opportunities
- Go / No-Go Recommendation
- 30-Day Action Plan

## Learning Outcomes

- Understanding startup validation
- Market sizing techniques
- Customer discovery
- Competitor benchmarking
- Risk analysis
- Strategic planning
- VC-style reporting

## Tools Used

- Claude AI
- Market Research Techniques
- Business Analysis Frameworks
