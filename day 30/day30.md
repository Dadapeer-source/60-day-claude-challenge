# 🚚 Supply Chain Builder

An interactive web application that teaches the fundamentals of supply chain management through real-world business decision making. Users build a complete supply chain by selecting suppliers, factory locations, warehouse strategies, transportation methods, and inventory policies while observing how each decision impacts business performance.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Supply Chain Builder is an educational simulation designed for beginners to understand how supply chains work. The application explains every concept before asking the user to make a decision, making it easy to learn logistics, sourcing, inventory management, transportation, and business trade-offs.

Each playthrough generates a new fictional company, creating a unique learning experience every time.

## 🎮 How It Works

1. Start the simulation.
2. A random company profile is generated.
3. Learn about each supply chain concept.
4. Choose your supplier strategy.
5. Select the factory location.
6. Choose a warehouse strategy.
7. Select the transportation method.
8. Decide the inventory strategy.
9. Observe how every decision changes the business metrics.
10. Review the final Supply Chain Dashboard and Overall Score.
11. Replay with a new company and optimize your strategy.
